Add items to the store

Enter the name of the product in the 'Product Name' field, current price of the product in 'Price' (modifiable later), specify the quantity, and indicate the location of the product. Divide the store into sections for each category and organize them sequentially to facilitate easy identification. Additionally, specify the section and exact position of the product for fast identification purposes in the designated 'Place' field.
